# WhatsNext Vision Motors – Salesforce CRM Project

## Project Overview

WhatsNext Vision Motors is an automotive company that developed a Salesforce CRM solution to organize and streamline its internal processes for vehicle sales, dealer coordination, test drives, and service-related requests. The CRM integrates automated workflows, validation rules, and custom interfaces to improve operational efficiency, enhance data accuracy, and deliver a smoother experience for both customers and staff.

---

## Features

Centralized management of vehicles, dealers, customers, orders, test drives, and service requests.

Automatic assignment of the nearest dealer to customer orders.

Stock validation to prevent orders for out-of-stock vehicles.

Automated email reminders for scheduled test drives.

Batch Apex and scheduled jobs to update order status and stock levels.

Custom Lightning App, page layouts, and reports for enhanced UI/UX.

---

## Future Enhancements

Several improvements are planned for future versions of the system, including:

* Integration of a chatbot for immediate customer support
* AI-based recommendations for assigning the most suitable dealer
* Connectivity with external inventory management platforms
* Deployment through Salesforce Mobile Publisher for mobile access
